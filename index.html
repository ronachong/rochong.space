How to quickly make more passable art if you don't actually have the skills

  • Draw from a reference image: it will help you because you don't know how to draw bodies without them.
  • After drawing from reference, draw it again because it won't be good enough the first time.
    Use your laptop as a lightbox to trace over what you drew, 'cause it's too hard without tracing. Ha ha.
  • Iterations make everything better!
  • O.K., now go take your improved drawing and scan it into the computer.
  • TRACE OVER THAT and it'll be like, 3X better.
  • Actually, it might take you one more time for it to be literally 3X better. But that's just if you want to be technical about it.
  • O.K. your drawing should be ready to go now. Go scan it and crop it in Illustrator so it look like a proper graphic, even though it's just a drawing. ^^
