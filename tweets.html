11/14/2015

It's the wee hours of 11/14/2015, the day before my application to Holberton is due (shout out to you guys if you're reading this!) and I'm kinda feeling wiped. Working hard late into the night to finish things off is a familiar feeling, and so is the feeling of sort of, but not really quite getting there (hello all my essays during school; not that I'd boast about it at a party). This can happen a lot if you're like me and think of pursuing a ton of different possibilities at once. Also, if you're like me, you dream up of all these grand, somewhat complicated or hard-to-get-at concepts, which doesn't make it any easier. So, I'm not the best at scaling down my ideas into something neatly executable, but I try ^^'.

Keeping with the theme, the idea behind this website is a site that's all over the place, but modular at the same time. It's been an idea I've gradually built up over the past couple of years, ever since I first thought of digital spaces as potential spaces for art (see Vectorpark for a good example! personal blogs are art, too). I figured I could use a website as a dumping ground for all my different compulsions and no one could really complain about it, since it's my space. (Well, technically, it's Holberton's space. I'm sorry, it was an awful impulse to kill two birds with one stone!) [1] Why not use a tumblr for that? Maybe I'm retro and I like the idea of ownership, but having to create your own format feels that much more creative. For me, part of the extra creativity is having all the parts link up and for different parts of the sites to show their overlap. No strict dichotomies here. Nope! [2]

Actually getting to pull those ideas off is another story, and I feel like I only kinda sorta got there, but that's life.

The idea behind this website is also pretty self-centered and personal, which is why it does feel kind of weird to have Holberton as my current audience in lieu of, say, a purely anonymous and hypothetical one. (I also apologize for any moments of inappropriate disclosure or humor.) On the plus side, I guess it offers a pretty good idea of me, so, it balances out, doesn't it?

Anyway, I could go on and on, but I also have an application to turn in lol >.<
so that's it for now-- I'll stop burning this figurative candle wick here.


[1] Compulsions: visual story-telling, offmychest posts, archival of personal likes, dislikes and thought, and online interaction. ;)

[2] Hence the impulse to visually nest homepage posts in large icons of the rest of the site (not 100% fulfilled, by the way)--among a dozen other things.
